About Me

Welcome! I'm J. Christopher Polanco, MD, a General Surgery resident with a passion for combining medical expertise with data science and epidemiology. Currently, I'm pursuing a PhD in Epidemiology, focusing on leveraging computational methods and big data analytics to improve surgical outcomes and public health understanding.

As a surgeon-scientist, I'm particularly interested in the intersection of surgical practice, epidemiological research, and computational methods. I'm committed to advancing epidemiological methods and machine learning to analyze large-scale health data, aiming to identify patterns that can improve surgical care and patient outcomes.

Through this blog, I share my journey in coding and data science, particularly focusing on:

  • Medical data analysis and visualization
  • Statistical programming in R and Python
  • Machine learning applications in healthcare
  • Epidemiological methods, in particular causal inference
